
# Workout Generation Rescue Plan — Final Synchronized Version

## Why
The current 21:05/21:25 UTC WOD generation runs during the global peak load window of the AI gateway, causing intermittent failures. We move it to **06:30/06:50 UTC** (09:30/09:50 Cyprus summer, 08:30/08:50 winter) — the calmest window — and switch from "build today's WOD just before midnight" to "every morning, pre-build **tomorrow's** WODs". At Cyprus midnight the archive job rolls today's WODs to the gallery and tomorrow's pre-built WODs automatically become "today" via the existing `generated_for_date` filter. **Zero downtime, zero last-minute AI calls.**

## New Daily Timeline (UTC / Cyprus summer)
```text
05:00 UTC / 07:00 CY  send-morning-notifications        (unchanged)
06:30 UTC / 09:30 CY  generate-wod-bodyweight-daily     → builds TOMORROW
06:50 UTC / 09:50 CY  generate-wod-equipment-daily      → builds TOMORROW
21:00 UTC / 00:00 CY  archive-old-wods                  (moved from 22:00)
22:05 UTC / 00:05 CY  generate-daily-ritual             (unchanged)
02:00 UTC             backup-wod-generation             (safety net, unchanged)
02:15 UTC             watchdog-wod-check                (safety net, unchanged)
```

## Switchover safety
- **Today's WOD already exists** — no user-visible gap on switchover day. Tomorrow morning the new 06:30 UTC job builds the day after.
- **DST-safe** — schedules stored in UTC; human strings show both summer/winter Cyprus times.
- **If morning generation fails** — backup at 02:00 UTC retries, watchdog at 02:15 UTC alerts admin, manual button always available.
- **Notifications safe** — 05:00 UTC morning notifications pull today's WOD which was pre-built the previous morning, so they always have content.
- **Stripe safe** — idempotency keys + cleanup logic unchanged; pre-generation just shifts when products are created, not how.
