---
name: Isometric Placement and Hypertrophy Rule
description: Static/isometric exercises are complementary only and must not interrupt Main/Finisher momentum or replace hypertrophy work
type: feature
---
Static/isometric exercises are complementary, not primary hypertrophy work. Do not place them inside Main Workout, circuit, EMOM, AMRAP, For Time, Tabata, or finisher momentum blocks unless the entire section is intentionally dedicated to isometrics.

Allowed placement: warm-up/activation, cool down, end-of-session accessory work, or a clearly named dedicated isometric section.

For hypertrophy programs, primary exercise slots must use dynamic loaded or rep-based movements that can progress toward technical failure. Do not use static holds such as back lever, front lever, L-sit, plank, dead hang, support hold, hollow hold, arch hold, wall sit, or isometric chest squeeze as working-set replacements.
