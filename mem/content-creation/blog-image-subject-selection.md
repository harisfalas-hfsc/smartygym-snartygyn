---
name: Blog Image Subject Selection
description: Featured blog images must literally match the article subject, demographic, movement, food, or topic.
type: feature
---
Blog featured images must be selected from the article title, excerpt, and body before category. Category is only fallback context.

If a title/body names a body part, movement, demographic, food, supplement, health marker, or training method, the image must show that exact subject in the foreground.

For glute articles, "glute" means butt/hips: use a correct hip-thrust/glute-bridge/lower-body hypertrophy visual where the glutes and hip extension are unmistakable. Do not use upper-body lifts, bars on shoulders/neck, head-hanging poses, generic gym scenes, treadmills, boxing, yoga, nutrition, or unrelated exercises.

If an image could be mistaken for a different subject than the article title, reject it and regenerate with a more literal prompt before publishing.
