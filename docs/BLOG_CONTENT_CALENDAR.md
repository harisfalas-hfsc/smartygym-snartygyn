## SEO Optimization Checklist (Every Article)

### On-Page SEO
- [ ] Primary keyword in title (H1)
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Keyword density: 1-2% (natural placement)
- [ ] Secondary keywords distributed throughout
- [ ] H2 and H3 subheadings with semantic keywords
- [ ] Meta title: 50-60 characters with primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150-160 characters with CTA
- [ ] URL slug: short, keyword-rich, hyphenated
- [ ] Image alt text: descriptive with keywords
- [ ] Internal links: 5-10 relevant links to SmartyGym pages
- [ ] External links: 2-4 authoritative sources
- [ ] Schema markup: Article, HowTo, or FAQPage
- [ ] Readability: Flesch score 60+, short paragraphs, bullet points

### Content Quality
- [ ] Minimum 1,800 words (ideal: 2,500+)
- [ ] Original content (no plagiarism)
- [ ] Evidence-based (cite scientific studies when applicable)
- [ ] Actionable advice (practical takeaways)
- [ ] Engaging introduction (hook reader in first 2 sentences)
- [ ] Clear conclusion with summary and CTA
- [ ] Visual elements: images, infographics, or embedded videos
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting

### Promotion & Distribution
- [ ] Share on social media (schedule posts)
- [ ] Email to newsletter subscribers
- [ ] Submit to fitness aggregators
- [ ] Outreach to backlink targets
- [ ] Repurpose as social media snippets

## Writer Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Expert but Accessible:** Write with authority but avoid jargon
- **Motivational:** Inspire readers to take action
- **Evidence-Based:** Reference scientific studies and research
- **Practical:** Provide actionable, implementable advice
- **Global Perspective:** Avoid region-specific references unless necessary

### Content Structure
1. **Hook:** Compelling opening question or statement
2. **Problem:** Define the reader's pain point or question
3. **Solution:** Provide expert guidance and actionable steps
4. **Proof:** Include evidence, research, or examples
5. **CTA:** Clear call-to-action linking to SmartyGym features

### Style Rules
- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences max)
- Include bulleted lists and numbered steps
- Add subheadings every 200-300 words
- Write in active voice
- Use contractions for conversational tone
- Include rhetorical questions to engage readers
- Define technical terms on first use
