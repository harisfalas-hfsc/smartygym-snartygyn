## üìù Content Guidelines

### Content Creation Principles

1. **Always Attribute to Haris Falas**
   - Workouts: "Designed by Haris Falas"
   - Programs: "Created by Sports Scientist Haris Falas"
   - Tips: "Coach Haris recommends..."
   - Never present content as system-generated

2. **Minimize AI References**
   - SmartyWorkout is a "personalized workout generator"
   - Uses "professional training protocols"
   - Based on "coaching methodology"
   - Only mention AI in technical contexts if necessary
   - NEVER position SmartyGym as "AI fitness coach"

3. **Emphasize Human Touch**
   - "Personally designed by..."
   - "Based on 20+ years coaching experience"
   - "Real expert guidance"
   - "Human understanding of your fitness journey"

4. **Science-Based Language**
   - Reference training principles (progressive overload, periodization)
   - Cite exercise science when appropriate
   - Use proper fitness terminology
   - Explain WHY, not just WHAT

5. **Accessibility Focus**
   - Write for 8th-10th grade reading level
   - Define technical terms on first use
   - Use bullet points for scannable content
   - Include visual aids (images, videos) where helpful

### Content Hierarchy

**Primary Content** (Highlight Haris's Expertise):
- Workout library (500+ workouts)
- Training programs (multi-week plans)
- Exercise library with coaching tips
- Educational blog articles

**Secondary Content** (Supporting Services):
- SmartyWorkout generator (complementary tool)
- Fitness calculators (BMR, 1RM, macros)
- Progress tracking tools
- Community features

**Marketing Focus Order**:
1. Expert-designed workout library
2. Structured training programs
3. Haris Falas's credentials and expertise
4. SmartyWorkout generator (as a bonus feature)
5. Community and support features
